Request Processing Procedure

1

Submit Request

Send an email or contact via GitHub with the subject "Personal Data Deletion Request" along with your identity verification information.

2

Identity Verification

We will verify your identity to ensure the request comes from the actual data owner. You may need to provide verification information such as registered email, full name, or other details.

3

Process Request

After successful verification, we will proceed to delete all your personal data from our systems within 30 business days.

4

Confirmation of Completion

We will send an email confirming that the data deletion has been completed and provide a detailed report of the data that has been deleted.

Required Information

To expedite the processing, please provide the following information in your request:

  • Full name
  • Registered account email
  • Reason for data deletion request (optional)
  • Additional information for identity verification (if needed)

Processing Time

We commit to processing your data deletion request as quickly as possible:

  • Initial response: Within 3-5 business days
  • Identity verification: 1-2 business days
  • Complete data deletion: Maximum 30 business days

Data That Will Be Deleted

When your data deletion request is approved, we will delete all:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account data and login information
  • Activity history and service usage
  • Analytics and personal statistics data
  • Cookies and related tracking data
  • Information from third-party social media services
